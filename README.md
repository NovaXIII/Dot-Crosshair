## 🎯 Dot Crosshair

A simple and clean mod that replaces the default crosshair with a minimal dot for better precision and focus.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎯 Minimal dot crosshair for improved accuracy  
- 🧼 Clean and distraction-free interface  
- ⚡ Lightweight and performance-friendly  
- 🔧 No setup required (plug & play)  
- 🎮 Perfect for PvP, building, and survival  

---

## 💡 Why use Dot Crosshair?

The default Minecraft crosshair can sometimes feel bulky or distracting.  
With a minimal dot, your aim becomes sharper and your screen stays clean — helping you focus exactly where it matters.

---

## 🚀 Perfect For

- PvP players who want better aim  
- Builders who need precise placement  
- Players who prefer a clean UI  

---

## 📦 Installation
  
1. Download the resourcepack
2. Place the `.zip` file into your `resourcepacks` folder  
3. Launch the game and enjoy!
